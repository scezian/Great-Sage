Chapter 1798: Chapter 1798 1796: Clash in the Black Abyss
============================================================

The many Yin-Yang dual origins interwoven and filled the leaves, Lu Ye immersed his mental consciousness, meticulous to the extreme.

This is the most intricate and complex spirit runes Lu Ye has encountered so far, compared to which, As-Close-As-Siblings does not even deserve to carry its shoes.

He must ensure flawless success throughout the construction process, without any errors; otherwise, all would be lost. The mystical properties of the Talent Tree afforded him the possibility to complete this; had he tried to construct such spirit runes on the jade tablet, he would have likely failed long ago.

Several days later, when the last basic element was successfully constructed, this Blood Eye Spirit Runes was truly complete.

Under internal observation, there was a prominent eye pattern on the lush leaves of the Talent Tree. This pattern was pieced together from several leaves, occupying a large area. From afar, it appeared as if the Talent Tree had grown an eye, with intertwined patterns within it; upon closer inspection, one could find an inverted triangle pattern at the pupil's position, looking extremely peculiar.

Lu Ye was very curious about the function of this spirit rune…

When the Demon Eye blinks, if the cultivator is in the marvel outside, their Divine Sea would be eroded by this Blood Eye, suspended in the Void. This Blood Eye has an illusory effect on the cultivator, greatly unsettling their mood and making them prone to anger; in severe cases, they might even be unable to control themselves.

But if the cultivator is within the Core World, under the Blood Moon, their power would noticeably increase.

According to Lu Ye's speculation, it's the Sacred Blood of the Blood Ancestor at work. In other words, whether the Blood Eye reflected in the cultivator's Divine Sea, or the Blood Moon suspended in this world, is an manifestation of the sacred blood's might in different environments.

Even though he managed to glimpse the Blood Eye Spirit Runes from it, he should not compare the power when activated to it, and it might even yield completely different effects because, after all, he is not the Blood Ancestor.

So he would need to find someone to test it on. With this thought, Lu Ye sent a message out, summoning Xiang Yin and sister.

More than ten days passed again.

On this day, while Lu Ye was meditating, the audio talisman shook.

Checking it, he found Hu Dequan had sent a message.

The time has come, the competition of the Black Abyss is about to begin, unaware, it has been a year since he came into this world.

Based on all the intelligence he has acquired, to completely crush the Sacred Blood Summit can only happen on the battlefield of Black Abyss. If this time fails, then he can only wait another decade.

A decade may not be much for other Moon Jade cultivators, but for Lu Ye, it's quite long.

So this competition can only be a victory, not a defeat!

He stood up, mental consciousness swelling, and walked out of his chamber.

Soon arriving outside, numerous figures revealed themselves from around; precisely the ten plus cultivators he had subdued earlier, most of them were Moon Jade, with two or three Constellations among them, some showing slight unease.

Not saying much, months of dormancy and preparation, everyone had been waiting for this day.

"Let's go!" Lu Ye led the way to the side, with numerous cultivators following closely. Shortly, on a pre-arranged Teleportation Array, light burst forth, this hidden base, the population dissipated.

A day later, in another hidden place, Lu Ye quietly waited with his forces.

After a while, footsteps approached.

The meditating Lu Ye turned his gaze and immediately saw some familiar faces, not only them, but around, some hidden presences appeared gradually.

Hu Dequan, Yang Ling, Meng Xu stopped at a distance from Lu Ye, mutually exchanging glances.

After a while, Hu Dequan spoke solemnly, "Brother Lu, we can trust you, right?"

Though several months of contact had been made, and occasionally personnel had been exchanged between both sides, when really at this step, the Red Talisman Society was still somewhat uneasy.

If Lu Ye's people were arranged by Lih Tian, then getting so close to them would inevitably lead to defeat in this battle.

Despite existing signs indicating this was unlikely.

"You don't seem to have any other choice." Lu Ye spoke.

Hu Dequan gave a bitter smile, this was precisely the toughest point for the Red Talisman Society.

Meng Xu spoke, "Actually, I've always had a question, could Fellow Daoist answer?"

Lu Ye looked at him, "Fellow Daoist, whatever you wish to ask."

Meng Xu said, "Fellow Daoist has mid-stage Moon Jade cultivation, how could you gather so many cultivators with higher cultivation than you to serve under you? They seem extremely loyal to you!"

Meng Xu had noticed this before, and he had also probed through Xiang Yin and others, but had never gotten an answer, hence always confused.

For cultivators, power is forever the voice of authority; Lu Ye has many late-stage Moon Jade cultivators here, in terms of cultivation higher than him, why do they obey him?

He hadn't directly asked Lu Ye before, it's people's secrets after all, probing rashly isn't right, but now with the battle impending, he needed clarity, for even if they trusted Lu Ye, whether Lu Ye's subordinates could be trusted was another matter. Moreover, when the battle starts, if someone on their side turned against them, the situation would be dire.

Lu Ye pondered briefly and said, "The Blood Race is of two kinds, one is ordinary Blood Race, one is with Sacred Blood, possessing saintliness, the latter can condense treasure blood, which has infinite uses and can be granted to other Blood Race, making them Blood Servants, Blood Servants would be inherently loyal to the Blood Master."

Meng Xu frowned, "So Xiang Yin and they are your Blood Servants... Yet Fellow Daoist's time in this world is short, by saintliness, they should surpass you, right?"

"Before coming to this realm, I already possessed saintliness, unlike Xiang Yin and the others."

With a confused expression, Meng Xu asked, "So are you a human race or a Blood Race?"

"I am naturally human. The Blood Race saintliness is just one of my powers; it doesn't affect me at all."

Meng Xu remained silent, unsure whether he believed it, but he did clarify the doubt in his heart. Xiang Yin and the others are actually Blood Servants...

Hu Dequan spoke up, "Alright, things have come to this point, and there's no turning back for any of us." He looked deeply at Lu Ye, "Brother Lu, I hope you won't disappoint us."

"I won't!"

Hu Dequan turned his head and glanced around before shouting in a low voice, "Let's begin!"

As he spoke, cultivators of the Red Talisman Society hidden around emerged one by one. Not many, just around thirty, adding Lu Ye and the others on this side, making a total of about forty.

This is the Red Talisman Society's foundation accumulated through countless hardships over the years, and the only available manpower the Society has.

The cultivators from both sides gathered together.

Hu Dequan raised his hand, summoning a streak of red light at his fingertips, with a fierce and violent aura emanating.

Not just him, but Yang Ling, Zhang Xu did the same as well.

The three simultaneously activated the might of the red talisman, causing the Void to begin trembling.

Lu Ye quietly observed from the side, having learned a lot about the Black Abyss Contest from the Red Talisman Society. He knew that Hu Dequan and the others weren't truly activating the red talismans but were using their power for a form of guidance.

The target of guidance, if Lu Ye's guess was correct, should be the Core World Consciousness!

Sure enough, as the power of the red talisman continued to spread, a small black dot suddenly appeared in the middle of the three. Initially imperceptible, it expanded fiercely in all directions in just an instant.

No one made any effort to dodge.

In a split second, the cultivators experienced a momentary darkness, feeling a sense of weightlessness, and when they regained their senses, they were no longer in the original location.

Lu Ye took the opportunity to survey the surrounding area, immediately feeling a strangely familiar sensation.

Countless floating meteorites traversed the Void, with floating lands of varying sizes interspersed. The environment was precisely what he had encountered when participating in the Black Abyss Martial Arts Meet at Mount Fangcun.

The so-called Black Abyss, indeed, bore no difference to the space he had entered back then. Lu Ye wasn't sure if it was the same Black Abyss.

Most likely, it isn't, because the Black Abyss Contest here occurs once every ten years, while the Dwarf Race's Martial Arts Meet spans a longer cycle. If it were indeed the same Black Abyss, there should be times of overlap.

If that were true... a bunch of unfamiliar Moon Jade cultivators suddenly appearing amidst a martial arts gathering of constellations would be utterly chaotic.

Yet, over the years, star constellations participating in the Martial Arts Meet at Mount Fangcun have never encountered such an event.

Lu Ye leaned more towards the idea that it was another space, albeit similar to where he had been before.

The only difference was a patch of bright light emanating from a not-so-distant location.

Turning his head to look, he saw a sphere resembling a small sun glowing brightly. On closer inspection, the sphere's surface even exhibited some faint blood hues.

"Move, hurry and move!" Hu Dequan's shout echoed, and he darted towards the direction of the small sun.

The others followed closely behind.

Xiang Yin and her companions gathered around Lu Ye, looking at him for direction.

"Follow the plan." Lu Ye instructed calmly, leading his group to follow the Red Talisman Society cultivators. The plan, essentially, was to assist the Red Talisman Society.

A short while later, over forty people gathered around the small sun, all standing solemnly. Hu Dequan looked towards Lu Ye and his group and solemnly opened his mouth, "Brother Lu, though we've communicated before, I must explain carefully that the Black Abyss Contest, for us, is the Protection Battle for the Origin!"

The "Origin" is what the Red Talisman Society calls the small sun; the Origin is the core world's Origin.

Furthermore, the Origin genuinely possesses its own consciousness, as Lu Ye confirmed during his prior discussion with Hu Dequan.

Previously, the core world's consciousness would have some interaction with Red Talisman Society's cultivators, providing guidance and assistance, but over time, this consciousness became increasingly silent, and often the Society's members received no response when seeking it.

The root cause was precisely the blood hue covering the Origin, from which Lu Ye clearly felt an intensely terrifying saintliness.

That blood hue undoubtedly stemmed from the power of the Blood Ancestor's treasure blood!

The treasure blood is gradually eroding the Origin, and the Origin is perpetually resisting, thus remaining in a dormant state.

Only during the Black Abyss Contest does the Origin Consciousness receive guidance from the might of the Red Talisman, which leads Red Talisman Society's cultivators into this location.

Chapter 1799: Chapter 1799 1797: Great Restoration
============================================================

In fact, initially, the cycle of Black Abyss Conflict was not once every ten years, but once every hundred years. Yet, as time passed, this cycle became shorter and shorter, until in recent times, it has changed to once every ten years.

Here is where the source consciousness hides, and the shortening of the conflict cycle undoubtedly indicates a dire situation for the source.

Judging by the current outcome, the erosion of the core world's origin by the Blood Ancestor's treasure blood has already achieved preliminary results. If it continues like this, even if the cultivators of the Red Talisman Society could still hold on, the source wouldn't sustain. If the source is completely devoured by the treasure blood, there would be no place for the cultivators of the Red Talisman Society in this realm.

"In the confrontation with the Sacred Blood Summit, we are at a disadvantage because they have larger numbers and Sun Blaze. But as the defending side, we have advantages too, because with the source's support, we arrived here early, giving us enough time to make some deployments!" Hu Dequan's voice continued to echo.

This is the biggest reason why, despite being at a disadvantage in numbers and overall strength, the Red Talisman Society can persist. Because every Black Abyss Conflict, they arrive in this space ahead of time to make some defensive deployments.

The source, ultimately being the source of the core world, naturally can use its rights to provide conveniences to its cultivators.

So, the current situation is that the cultivators of the Red Talisman Society arrived here first, and after some time, the people of the Sacred Blood Summit could open the passage to enter this place.

The duration of this time gap depends on how long the source consciousness can last. Because the process of the Sacred Blood Summit opening the passage is breaking the blockade that the source consciousness has on this space.

"Therefore, we must deploy as many defensive methods as possible. Once the battle begins, the primary task is to protect the source, and secondly, to kill the enemy! After the war starts, the source consciousness will repel all invading enemies. As long as we can hold on long enough, the cultivators of the Sacred Blood Summit will be expelled from this place, and that is how we can achieve victory!"

Hu Dequan had discussed this with Lu Ye before, and had mentioned how the repulsion had weakened over time. Initially, the source consciousness needed only a day, even half a day, to repel the enemies from this space. But during the last Black Abyss Conflict, it took five full days.

This time will certainly be longer.

The reason, after all, is that the source consciousness's control over this space is weakening, and this undoubtedly poses a disadvantage for the defending side of the Red Talisman Society.

After a brief explanation of the essence of Black Abyss Conflict, the cultivators quickly sprang into action, and they must perform more deployments before the Sacred Blood Summit's cultivators arrive.

Figures shuttled back and forth, transporting large amounts of meteorites and floating land, while cultivators proficient in formations began laying arrays on these meteorites and floating lands.

Lu Ye did not rush to help immediately but instead flew towards the source glowing with light.

As early as when he was amid the Demon Eye Marvel, Lu Ye had surmised that the core world has its consciousness. Because the core world, during its mutual pursuit with him, indeed showed conscious reactions, and he even attempted to communicate with it but received no response, ultimately being swallowed by the core world.

After entering this realm, Lu Ye similarly tried communicating with the consciousness of this realm, yet all to no avail.

Until today, he finally realized that the consciousness of the core world has always been hiding in this special space, so naturally, won't respond to his calls.

But if this is the case, then what was the situation previously when the core world approached him? At that time, the core world was clearly acting consciously, searching for his whereabouts...

Especially when he attempted to escape and sacrificed the treasure blood, the core world reacted exceedingly fiercely.

In his musings, a chilling thought suddenly sprang into Lu Ye's mind—what if the consciousness that the core world exhibited then wasn't the source?

In other words, what if the act of swallowing him into the core world wasn't spearheaded by the source consciousness, but another consciousness? It perceived his Blood Race lineage, swallowed him, and transformed him into its aid...

If so, then the truth is somewhat terrifying, and the situation facing the Red Talisman Society is far more serious than imagined.

Lu Ye's expression changed, as he raised his hand to touch the luminous source in front of him.

Not far away, Hu Dequan observed coldly.

The action of Lu Ye flying towards the core source was clear to him, yet he did not intervene, knowing full well that any cultivator with saintliness approaching the source faces no good consequences. This had been proven in previous battles.

Because the thin layer of blood light covering the source seems particularly intense with saintliness, any cultivator with saintliness finds their strength increasingly suppressed the closer they get to the source.

Yet, to Hu Dequan's amazement, Lu Ye showed no signs of suppression, even as he extended his hand to touch the source, causing Hu Dequan's eyes to widen.

What is happening?

He couldn't comprehend, as the scene before him already surpassed his established perceptions.

It's certain that Lu Ye possesses saintliness, and it's quite intense, yet it shouldn't be intense enough not to be suppressed by that layer of blood light...

But peculiarly, such an event just happened.

He felt peculiar because he hadn't understood Lu Ye's prior statements.

Lu Ye had told him before that while he does possess saintliness and can freely wield various secret techniques of the Blood Race, saintliness is merely one of his powers!

This power will surely be suppressed by the source's external blood light, as it carries the force of the Blood Ancestor's Treasure Blood, its saintliness far beyond Lu Ye's reach.

But Lu Ye possesses not only saintliness; he himself is also a human race combat cultivator. Standing here, he indeed cannot activate his own saintliness, and the Blood Race's secret techniques are difficult to perform. Yet, his mana remains unaffected.

If it were Xiang Yin, she might not even be able to approach the Origin.

Hu Dequan, seeing the situation wasn't right, wanted to step forward to stop it, but in the next instant, his eyes widened significantly.

Because as Lu Ye's large hand touched the Origin, the hazy blood light surrounding it started to undergo some strange changes. Originally, this blood light was like a layer of shell tightly enveloping the Origin, but now it seemed to be boiling.

At this moment, extremely tiny, almost undetectable roots extended from Lu Ye's palm, in the blink of an eye wrapping the large Origin.

Lu Ye activated the power of the Talent Tree!

If the Origin is seen as a cultivator, then the hazy blood light is like an intrusion into the cultivator's body, similar to the curse poison in the merfolk race bloodline, akin to the Soul-Devouring Aphid in Yaya's Divine Sea back then.

Lu Ye activated the Talent Tree's power to see if he could refine away this blood light.

In just a moment of contact, Lu Ye knew this could be done!

Because with the activation of the Talent Tree's power, the layer of blood light had some abnormal reactions.

The blood light transformed into blood mist, as if wanting to evaporate, and the color of the blood light steadily weakened.

Hu Dequan finally came to his senses, leaped to stand in front of Lu Ye, suspicious and uncertain: "Brother Lu, what are you doing?"

He had wanted to stop it just now because whatever Lu Ye is doing, the Origin must not be touched casually. If Lu Ye harbored ill intentions, the battle would be lost without even fighting.

But having seen the change in the Origin, he realized that what Lu Ye was doing seemed to be good for the Origin.

"Talk later," Lu Ye replied, focusing wholeheartedly on activating the power of the Talent Tree. At this moment, he keenly sensed the refining process of the blood light causing some profound essence to be absorbed into his body through the roots of the Talent Tree.

He felt his own saintliness rapidly enhancing!

Originally, after arriving in this realm, he had bestowed much treasure blood, which had actually led to the loss of his own saintliness. However, due to his saintliness being too intense, this damage was not very noticeable. Compared to other members of the Blood Race, he still had enough advantage to crush them in terms of saintliness.

To supplement the loss of saintliness, he would have to refine more Blood Race Sacred Blood.

He certainly couldn't find Blood Race Sacred Blood for the time being, yet unexpectedly, he found a huge supplement here!

As he pondered, Lu Ye realized that this blood light is the manifestation of the Blood Ancestor's treasure blood. His intent was merely to refine it away, thereby relieving the pressure on the Origin Consciousness, maybe enabling it to assist his side in the upcoming battle.

But this process of refining turned it into cultivation resources similar to spirit jade.

In the refining process of spirit jade, the roots of the Talent Tree probe into the spirit jade, refining impurities while absorbing the energy for oneself, strengthening one's own foundation.

Currently, the spirit jade is replaced by this layer of blood light; essentially his present state is no different from normal cultivation.

It's just that in this cultivation, his cultivation level is not increased; what grows rapidly is his Blood Race saintliness.

Huge supplement!

The previously lost saintliness was quickly replenished, and it continued to enhance.

Hu Dequan stood aside and watched, increasingly confirming that Lu Ye's actions were beneficial to the Origin, so he no longer had the thought of stopping him, leaving him here, and started overseeing various matters of defense construction.

Lu Ye originally thought he could quickly refine that layer of blood light cleanly. Who knew regardless of his refining efforts, that layer of blood light showed no signs of weakening.

But the growth of his internal saintliness was visibly discernible.

This led him to gradually understand one thing — it was not only the surface of the Origin that was stained by the Blood Ancestor's sacred blood, but internally as well, giving him the illusion that it couldn't be thoroughly refined.

If given enough time, he should be able to restore the Origin to its original state, but currently, there isn't enough time, which posed a dilemma.

Just as he was feeling helpless, he suddenly sensed a force gently touching the protective barrier of his Divine Sea, as if someone was knocking on the door. Lu Ye paused momentarily, quickly realized and hurriedly opened his Divine Sea, urgently immersing himself, manifesting his psyche spirit body.

Inside the Divine Sea, Lu Ye raised his eyes and looked, immediately seeing a new figure here.

An old man with white hair, leaning on a cane.

The figure was very short, roughly the height of a ten-year-old child, with a long beard hanging down to his chest, and a forehead with series of large protruding lumps, resembling mountain peaks.

Chapter 1800: Chapter 1800 1798: Origin Consciousness

Looking at the small old man who suddenly appeared in his Divine Sea, and recalling the slight knocking sensation he felt in his Divine Sea earlier, Lu Ye immediately realized the identity of the other party: "Origin Consciousness?"

If he wasn't mistaken, the small old man in front of him was the same type of existence as Number Nine and Flower Girl, both manifestations of the will of a domain.

The protection of the Divine Sea being triggered was undoubtedly the handiwork of the other party.

The small old man nodded slightly: "Correct!" He then asked: "Dao Tree lineage?"

"What?" Lu Ye frowned, not quite understanding.

The small old man gave Lu Ye a deep look but didn't explain further, though Lu Ye clearly saw a hint of apprehension in the other's eyes, unsure if it was his imagination.

Just as he was about to ask more, the small old man seemed to discover a new world, staring at the seven-color Divine Lotus beneath Lu Ye's psyche spirit body in surprise: "Warm Divine Lotus?" Upon closer inspection, he shook his head: "Not quite right, just a doppelganger. Boy, what is your relationship with the Soul Race?"

"Barely count as half a member of the Soul Race," Lu Ye answered casually.

The small old man's eye visibly twitched. What was this? What does it mean to barely count as half a member of the Soul Race? Either you are or you aren't, there's no half about it. But seeing such a Divine Lotus in the other's Divine Sea, he knew his relationship with the Soul Race was not ordinary; otherwise, he couldn't have such a thing guarding his Divine Sea, something not everyone could possess.

While deep in thought, Lu Ye spoke up: "Veteran Fang, the Black Abyss Contest has already started. The people from Sacred Blood Summit should attack soon. I wish to wipe them out with this battle. Can you provide any assistance?"

The small old man was momentarily stunned, realizing that "Veteran Fang" referred to him, undoubtedly derived from the name Mount Fangcun.

No one had ever called him that before; he was a bit surprised, but not too concerned. Upon hearing this, he slowly shook his head: "The Origin has been tainted by the treasure blood of the Blood Ancestor. For many years, I've been fighting against it. Without your help today, I wouldn't have been able to meet you here. Once the contest begins, all I can do is repel the intruders with all my might; beyond that, I cannot offer any assistance."

Lu Ye understood, feeling a bit disappointed, but upon closer thought, he couldn't be too demanding. The Origin had been contaminated by the power of the Blood Ancestor's treasure blood for so many years. Today, with his help, Veteran Fang's Origin Consciousness could relax for a moment, but it couldn't be relaxed forever.

Veteran Fang noticed Lu Ye's uniqueness, which is why he chose to appear and meet him.

So, in the matter of the Black Abyss Contest, it was up to him.

"Boy, don't expect too much too quickly. Win this battle first, then help me fully resolve the crisis within the Origin, so we can plan for the future!" Veteran Fang's voice echoed.

The crisis within the Origin had only been slightly alleviated, not completely resolved. Achieving that wasn't a one-day task.

The Origin, contaminated by part of the power of the Blood Ancestor's treasure blood, was like falling sick. As the saying goes, an illness strikes like a mountain falling, but leaves like silk unwinding…

From Veteran Fang's perspective, it was unrealistic for Lu Ye to expect to eliminate Sacred Blood Summit in one battle; such matters required gradual effort.

Lu Ye frowned; he didn't want to plan for the future; he only wished to decide the battle's outcome here and now.

Thinking it over, Lu Ye asked: "Veteran Fang, does the Blood Ancestor's treasure blood have its own consciousness?"

The reason for this suspicion naturally stemmed from the various thoughts that had flashed through his mind earlier. If the consciousness that pulled him into this realm wasn't Mount Fangcun's, then there was only one other possibility.

Veteran Fang nodded: "That's correct. However, there's no need to worry too much. That consciousness is not complete. It was born from the will left behind in the Blood Ancestor's treasure blood from years ago, so its spiritual intelligence is somewhat primitive, like a child who hasn't been born long. Moreover, this place is the Origin Space; it cannot enter, only you cultivators can."

Having received confirmation from Veteran Fang, even though Lu Ye had long suspected, he was still shocked.

He couldn't help but recall some information he obtained when he first encountered the Blood Race heritage. It was rumored that the Blood Race could, at the depth of their bloodline cultivation, achieve Drop of Blood Rebirth.

He always thought such things were too farfetched, but now it seemed he had been like a frog in a well.

The Blood Ancestor had been dead for who knows how many years, and her remains divided into many pieces, yet a drop of treasure blood she left behind here had given birth to consciousness…

Just one drop of the Blood Ancestor's treasure blood had left Mount Fangcun's core consciousness so embarrassed; how powerful must the Blood Ancestor have been in her time? And what kind of existence could have torn the Blood Ancestor apart?

Lu Ye couldn't allow himself to ponder further.

As for now, since that consciousness was nurtured by the will left in the treasure blood of the Blood Ancestor, it had certainly inherited many things from the Blood Ancestor. Could this be considered a form of rebirth?

Veteran Fang said the Blood Ancestor's drop of treasure blood couldn't enter this place. As for why, Lu Ye wasn't sure, but since Veteran Fang said so, it must be true. In other words, that drop of treasure blood had always been outside… Lu Ye immediately thought of the Blood Moon hanging in the sky!

He had always been curious about the nature of that Blood Moon and had speculated before. Now, hearing Veteran Fang's words, he could almost confirm that the Blood Moon was indeed transformed from the treasure blood of the Blood Ancestor.

However, Lu Ye quickly remembered another issue: "Veteran Fang, if the Blood Ancestor's treasure blood can't enter, how was the Origin contaminated?"

"Naturally, it's been contaminated by the saintliness within the cultivators who died here. Those saintliness, in essence, are also extensions of the Blood Ancestor's treasure blood's power! The naive will of the treasure blood had always aimed to occupy the Origin, and all saintliness powers would autonomously contaminate the Origin."

Lu Ye suddenly understood. For generations, the battles at the Black Abyss surely caused numerous cultivators of the Sacred Blood Summit to perish. After their deaths, the saintliness within them dispersed and slowly infiltrated the Origin.

"So, young one, do your best to win this round. Afterwards, this old man will join forces with you to figure out how to handle that drop of treasure blood." If it were anyone else here, Veteran Fang would never have appeared because it would be meaningless. But Lu Ye was different. The Dao Tree lineage gave Veteran Fang hope, as to the Dao Tree lineage, everything in the world could be refined, including the Blood Ancestor's treasure blood!

Lu Ye nodded, "I'll do my best!"

In the Divine Sea, the figure of Veteran Fang faded away.

Lu Ye opened his eyes, still pressing his hand on the Origin, urging the Talent Tree's power to continuously refine the treasure blood's strength within the Origin.

The more he refined, the lighter the pressure on the Origin Consciousness would become.

Everyone else was busy without pause, with Hu Dequan coordinating in all directions, continuously issuing various commands. All around the vast space of the Origin, formations overlapped, and hidden dangers lurked.

Until a few days later, this area of space suddenly began to tremble incessantly.

Only then did Hu Dequan flash to Lu Ye's side, "Brother, the people from Sacred Blood Summit are coming. According to the plan, we need to hide the Origin."

Though this concealment couldn't last long, and the cultivators of Sacred Blood Summit would eventually find it, delaying time as the defensive side was undoubtedly a strategy.

Lu Ye understood and stepped back.

With Hu Dequan's call, several cultivators immediately leapt over to set up a concealment array near the Origin.

The space shook continuously, stopping only after nearly half a day.

Hiding on a meteorite, aided by the formation to mask his aura, and simultaneously enhancing with Breath-concealing Spirit Runes, Lu Ye instantly detected a powerful mental consciousness sweeping over from a certain direction.

The mental consciousness of Lih Tian!

Surely, the cultivators of Sacred Blood Summit were coming out in full force, as they did every time, but every time they were thwarted and returned empty-handed. This contest had gone on for countless years since ancient times.

In the silent void, hidden dangers were lurking.

Soon, on Lih Tian's orders, the cultivators of the Sacred Blood Summit split into squads and spread out.

Their first task was to find the exact location of the Origin, as only then could they act purposefully.

As they searched, it was also a process of mutual maneuvering between both sides. The main objective of the Red Talisman Society was indeed to buy time, but under this overarching goal, eliminating enemies whenever possible was also essential.

In an instant, there were clusters of Blood Sea in the Origin space, searching in all directions. Within the Blood Sea, the cultivators of Sacred Blood Summit concealed themselves.

On the meteorite where Lu Ye was hiding, Hu Dequan was right beside him, looking tense and anxious: "Brother, how confident are you?"

In the past at this stage, the cultivators on the Red Talisman Society's side would generally choose to remain hidden, as their overall strength was inferior to that of their opponents, making a proactive attack unfavorable.

Moreover, the cultivators of Sacred Blood Summit would collaboratively use the Blood Sea Technique, which allowed them to attack when encountering an enemy and defend when under attack, making them quite difficult to deal with.

Unless the enemy just happened to enter the range of the formation they set up, they would leverage the formation's power to launch an attack. However, the Origin space was vast, and even though they had arrived several days earlier, the formations they could set were limited. Getting the enemy to wander into the range of the formations depended entirely on luck.

But this time was different. After discussing with Hu Dequan, Lu Ye had decided to adopt a more aggressive strategy.

"Just wait," Lu Ye replied. In his perception, the positions of all Blood Servants in the Origin space were clear at a glance, thanks to his bloodline perception rather than mental consciousness, allowing for a vast range of detection.

Hu Dequan felt a bit uneasy. Although he had been convinced by Lu Ye earlier, now that they reached this point, he couldn't help but feel tense. The Red Talisman Society didn't have many people, and couldn't afford to be too reckless.

In another part of the space, a group of six from the Red Talisman Society, along with Xiang Yin and her sister and two other Blood Servants who had later joined under Lu Ye's command, ten people in total, were rushing openly in one direction.

Xiang Yin and her sister led the charge, holding their audio talisman and constantly communicating with Lu Ye to confirm the direction.

Before long, Xiang Yin exclaimed, "Prepare to engage the enemy!"

The others in the squad looked up to see a Blood Sea ahead rapidly advancing, carefully searching every area they passed, attempting to locate the Origin's position.

Chapter 1801: Chapter 1801 1799: Something Out of the Ordinary
============================================================

The aura of Xiang Yin and her companions was not hidden, so when they discovered their enemies, the enemies also noticed their presence, causing the Sacred Blood Summit squad to rush over as if energized.

This is the habitual mindset at play, because in past Black Abyss struggles, once the Red Talisman Society cultivators faced the Sacred Blood Summit troops in such situations, they mostly chose to flee, and even if they didn't, they mainly focused on containment.

However, what surprised this Sacred Blood Summit squad was that the enemy's reaction was quite strange this time; they did not flee, nor did they intend to scatter for containment, but instead charged straight at them.

The distance between them quickly closed; both sides had the momentum to take down each other in one go. The lead Sacred Blood Summit cultivator felt odd, but it was too late to change formations by then.
